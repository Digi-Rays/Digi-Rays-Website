DAS - Digital Accounting System (Accounting Made Easy!)
DAS is a user-friendly accounting software for small and medium size business setups that helps simplify recording of business transactions and provides management with tools to enable them to control their business in a very productive way. It helps running a business easily, smoothly, feasibly and efficiently! DAS can save you from lots of stress, botheration and frustration as you will have complete control on the financial aspect of your business opertions!!
DAS is not only POS (Point-of-Sale) software but it caters to all business needs such as inventory management, A/C Payables/Receivables, Expenditures, Credit/Debit notes, Investments, Bank/Cash Accounts Management, Purchase Management, Sales Management, Ledger Accounts, etc. It has over 50 different reports, including, Income Statement and Balance Sheet help in taking business decisions instantly.
Main features include;
  • User friendly interface
  • Simplifies recording of business transaction
  • Extremely quick search for data
  • Provides instant reports for decision making
  • Auto pointing out of errors and ommissions
  • Ability to store/access printed documents digitally for quick reference
  • Remote access possible
  • Data Security & automated backups
  • No requirement for internet connectivity
  • Pay once only. No monthly/yearly installments.
