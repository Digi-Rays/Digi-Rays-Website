DAS - Digital Accounting System (Accounting Made Easy!)
DAS is a user-friendly accounting software for small and medium size business setups that helps simplify recording of business transactions and provides management with tools to enable them to control their business in a very productive way. It helps running a business smoothly, feasibly and efficiently! DAS can save a person from lots of unnecessary stress, botheration and frustration, as one has complete control on the financial aspect of business opertions!!
DAS is not only POS (Point-of-Sale) software but it caters to all business needs such as inventory management, A/C Payables/Receivables, Expenditures, Credit/Debit notes, Investments, Bank/Cash Accounts Management, Purchase Management, Sales Management, Ledger Accounts, Quotations Management, etc. It has over 50 different reports, including, Income Statement and Balance Sheet that help in taking business decisions instantly.
Main features include;
  • User friendly interface
  • Simplifies recording of business transactions
  • Extremely quick search for data/information
  • Provides instant reports for decision making
  • Auto pointing out of errors and ommissions
  • Ability to store/access printed documents digitally for quick reference
  • Remote access possible
  • Data Security & automated backups
  • No requirement for internet connectivity
  • Pay once only. No monthly/yearly installments
We are very optimistic that once DAS is implemented successfully, it can make business operations very smooth and efficient, resulting in not only financial savings but also time!
